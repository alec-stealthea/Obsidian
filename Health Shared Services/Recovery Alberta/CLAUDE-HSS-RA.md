## In-Flight Initiatives

**Compassionate Intervention Release of Information (CI RoI)**
The primary active initiative. Alberta's Compassionate Intervention Act (Bill 53, royal assent May 2025) requires rapid access to patient health information when a CI request is received. The CI RoI solution integrates the vendor's CMI app (CIP, hosted in AWS) with Epic Connect Care via external Akana (PHIE) and IBM ACE (RIE) middleware. As of June 2026, the POC hello-world handshake is underway. See [[CLAUDE-CI]] for full detail.

**Vacancy and Waitlist Management — Supported Living**
A VWM capability for Supported Living placements under Recovery Alberta (distinct from the Continuing Care VWM under Assisted Living Alberta). Minimal content in the vault to date — the `VWM Supported Living/` subfolder exists but has no detailed deliverables yet.

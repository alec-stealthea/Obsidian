There is a lot of research that shows that breast cancer screening leads to an improvement of quality adjusted life years for women.  There is however a real cost to managing an effective screening program.  An overview from Peter Attia, [[Beyond the mammogram a framework for smarter breast cancer screening]] provides some recommendations.

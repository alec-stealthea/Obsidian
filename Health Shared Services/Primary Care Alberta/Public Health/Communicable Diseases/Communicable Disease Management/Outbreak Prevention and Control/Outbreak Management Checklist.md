# Outbreak Management Checklist

Acute Care Outbreak Management Team (OMT)
Discussion Checklist
For sites that are combined acute care and congregate settings, application of acute care
outbreak measures to non-acute care areas in the same site will be determined by the OMT on a
case-by-case basis. Considerations include (but are not limited to) physical layout, staffing and
shared spaces and/or activities between patients and residents.

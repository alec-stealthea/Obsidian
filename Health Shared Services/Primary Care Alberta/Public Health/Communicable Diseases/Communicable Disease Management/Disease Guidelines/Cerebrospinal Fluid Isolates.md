## Case Definition
## Confirmed Case
Laboratory evidence of an organism, not otherwise reportable to Alberta Health, isolated from cerebrospinal fluid collected
from an individual who is not known to be HIV positive and who:
- has not sustained a penetrating or traumatic central nervous system injury
OR
- has not sustained a penetrating or traumatic vertebral column injury
OR
- does not have a central nervous system or vertebral column shunt

## Reporting Requirements
Laboratories
All laboratories shall report all positive laboratory results by mail, fax or electronic transfer within 48 hours (two business days)
to the:
- Chief Medical Officer of Health (CMOH) (or designate), and
- MOH (or designate) of the zone.
Alberta Health Services and First Nations and Inuit Health Branch
- Report outbreaks only using the Alberta Outbreak Report Form.
- Completion of a Notifiable Disease Report is not required.

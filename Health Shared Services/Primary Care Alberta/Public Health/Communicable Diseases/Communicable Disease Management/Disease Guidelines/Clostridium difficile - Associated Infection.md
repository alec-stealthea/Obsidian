## Case Definition
## Confirmed Case
Clinical illness (i.e., acute onset of diarrhea(A) or fever, abdominal pain and/or ileus) without another etiology and one of the
following:
- Laboratory confirmation of a positive toxin A or B assay for Clostridium difficile(B) (e.g., EIA, NAT for toxin A/B or tissue
culture assay)
OR
- Diagnosis of pseudomembranous colitis on endoscopy
OR
- Histological/pathological diagnosis of C. difficile(B) – Associated Infection (CDI)
(A) Diarrhea is defined as watery bowel movement (BM) that conforms to the shape of the container that holds it or BM that is unusual/different
for that patient.
(B) Also called Clostridioides difficile.

## Reporting Requirements
Laboratories
All laboratories shall report all positive laboratory results (including histological/pathological reports) by mail, fax or electronic
transfer to the:
- Chief Medical Officer of Health (CMOH) (or designate), and
- MOH (or designate) of the zone.
Alberta Health Services and First Nations and Inuit Health Branch
- Laboratory surveillance only.
- Completion of a Notifiable Disease Report form is not required.

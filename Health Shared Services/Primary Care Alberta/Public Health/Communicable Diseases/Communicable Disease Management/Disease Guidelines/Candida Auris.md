## Case Definition
## Confirmed Case
Any individual(A) with confirmatory laboratory evidence of Candida auris based on a culture or a culture independent diagnostic
test (CIDT) (e.g., polymerase chain reaction).(1)
(A) The individual would be considered a case regardless of whether the isolate is from an active clinical specimen or that used for the purpose
of screening for colonization.

## Reporting Requirements
Laboratories
All laboratories shall report all positive laboratory results by mail, fax or electronic transfer within 48 hours (two business days)
to the:
- Chief Medical Officer of Health (CMOH) (or designate), and
- MOH (or designate) of the zone.
Alberta Health Services and First Nations and Inuit Health Branch
- Report outbreaks only using the Alberta Outbreak Report Form.
- Completion of a Notifiable Disease Report is not required.

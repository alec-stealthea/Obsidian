The following is a draft of core values from Lisa that eventually became the [[Life Purpose and Core Values]]

CREATING: doing anything creative - writing - music - making something
beautiful from nothing
SHARING: being a Team - Being engaged in starting meaningful, successful
initiatives - something bigger than me - connection with humans - intent &amp;
passion &amp; you pulled it off - creating with others - collaborative, creative
process - learning together - have to be a team - building relationships -
common purpose - going along for a journey - getting to know people -
camaraderie - enjoying food together - cooking - making good food -
experiencing new places &amp; flavours - shared experience
OPENING PANDORA’S BOX: Being a part of other peoples change -
helping others - coaching - mentoring - managing - giving back - making a
difference - enthusiastically involved in helping others - actively listening -
opening pandora’s box - learning new things - being open - watching that for
others - journey of discovery - Transformation as a leader - i pick up
patterns - i can see what needs to be done and how things are connected -
actively listening to people - mental framework &amp; pathways to be able to do
it quickly - seeing an individual grow

MAKE EPIC MISTAKES: celebrate the mess ups - not overthinking - the
opportunity for learning - be tragically wrong - mistakes happen - the
learning is in how you recover from them - the performance that happens
with things go wrong - you learn a lot - the fear of the mistake is always
bigger that the outcome of the mistake - give yourself the opportunity to fail
- putting yourself out there - being unapologetic -
GIT ER DONE: high performer - putting 100% of myself into it - more is
more - getting doing things - being efficient - going all in - accomplishing
things - feeling useful

## Projected retirement spending

Retirement spending usually isn't flat. Model three phases:

| Phase | Years (approx) | Annual spend | Reasoning |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Go-go** (active, more travel) | first ~10 yrs |  | Often *higher* than working-life baseline |
| **Slow-go** (less travel) | next ~10 yrs |  | Typically declines |
| **No-go** (later years, more care) | final years |  | Travel down, healthcare up |

### Spending changes at retirement
What disappears or appears once you stop working:

- Removed: commuting, work wardrobe, employment-related costs, EI/CPP contributions, RRSP saving, mortgage (if paid off — see file 05)
- Added: private health/dental premiums (file 09), more travel/hobbies, possibly higher home/auto use

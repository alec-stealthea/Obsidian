### Spending changes at retirement
What disappears or appears once you stop working:

- Removed: commuting, work wardrobe, employment-related costs, EI/CPP contributions, RRSP saving, mortgage (if paid off — see file 05)
- Added: private health/dental premiums (file 09), more travel/hobbies, possibly higher home/auto use

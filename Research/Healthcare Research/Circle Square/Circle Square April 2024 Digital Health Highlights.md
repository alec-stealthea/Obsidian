In the United States, the number of independent physician practices has declined to 41.5% of the marketplace and more physicians working for hospitals/health systems (77.6%). The overall number of physicians employed grew by 5.1% from 2022 - 2023.  https://www.physiciansadvocacyinstitute.org/PAI-Research/PAI-Avalere-Study-on-Physician-Employment-Practice-Ownership-Trends-2019-2023

Concierge care is trending up with faster access to physicians and other care services tied to a retainer annual fee.

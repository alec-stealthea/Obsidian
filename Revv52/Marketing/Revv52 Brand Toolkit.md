> Guidance on how to best express Revv52 to our fans

## Understanding the Revv52 Brand

A brand is experiential. It's what people think of when Revv52 is seen or mentioned. Our brand is the perception people have about what we do and how we do it. The Revv52 mission, vision, and values are expressed in our brand.

## Mission and Vision

| | |
|---|---|
| **Mission** | Revv52 expands the boundaries of choral music by showcasing quality and innovative musical experiences for our audiences, while nurturing a love of music and fostering a caring environment among our members. |
| **Vision** | To be recognized as a leader in Canada's vocal and performing arts communities, fusing a variety of creative elements to produce performances that entertain and open the hearts and minds of audiences everywhere. |

> [!note] Updated Vision
> The [[Revv52 Draft Strategic Plan 2026-2028]] refines the vision to: *"Canada's leader in innovative vocal arts."*

## Core Values

As a group we value creativity, diversity, excellence, collaboration, and community.

**Guiding Principles:**

- We are artistically agile
- We are mindful of our Revv52 family
- Our brand is widely recognized
- We understand the evolving landscape
- We cherish our sense of fun
- The audience experience is dazzling
- Our structure is sustainable

## Strategic Goals and Initiatives

The following strategic initiatives were developed as part of the 2022 Strategic Planning Sessions (see also [[Revv52 Strategic Plan 2022-2025]]):

1. **Increasing Diversification and Discovery**
   - Internal and external diversification
   - Increased collaboration with different cultural communities
   - Diversity and inclusion committee

2. **Enhancing Collaboration and Relationships**
   - Form a relationship team
   - Actively seek out relationships
   - Inter-group collaboration

3. **Member Development**
   - Enhance vocal coaching
   - Introduce workshops and master classes
   - Enhance communication and connection

## Logo, Font, Colours and Internet Presence

### Logo Usage Guidelines

- Default logo colour is solid black
- A minimum clearance measuring half the height of the logo's lower case letters is recommended
- The tagline "Canada's Vocal Energy" should be included whenever possible
- Omit the tagline if the logo is smaller than 1" wide

### Brand Voice

**Words to describe Revv52:** Inspiring, Energized, Entertaining, Professional, Fun, Calgary, Singers, Performers, Popular Music

### Fonts

| Font | Usage |
|------|-------|
| **Lato Normal** | Primary body text |
| **Pathway Gothic One** | Secondary / Google Fonts open-source |
| **Trade Gothic Bold** | Logo font (available through Adobe) |

**Logo rendering within text:**
- Revv52 (shrinking numbers) — used within text to avoid line spacing issues
- Revv52 (enlarging letters) — only at the beginning of a paragraph or in a title

### Colour Palette

The colour palette for each show can change quite dramatically. The standard Revv52 colour palette is black and white with shades of grey with an accent red that matches the logo.

| Colour | Hex | RGB |
|--------|-----|-----|
| Light Grey | `#F0F0F0` | 245, 245, 245 |
| Medium Light Grey | `#DCDCDC` | 220, 220, 220 |
| Medium Grey | `#787878` | 120, 120, 120 |
| Dark Grey / Black | `#282828` | 40, 40, 40 |
| **Accent Red** | `#C71D22` | 199, 29, 34 |

### Platform-Specific Notes

#### Facebook
- Profile Photo: 170 x 170
- Cover Photo: 1,200 x 628
- Platform is changing to mirror other social channels — stay on top of changes
- Watermark photos with logo where they are Revv52 pictures

#### Instagram
- Profile Picture: 320 x 320
- Post images: 1,080 wide — Square (1:1) preferred, Portrait (4:5) 1,350, Landscape (1.91:1) 566
- Content mirrors FB posts plus short video clips (20-30 seconds) and Stories
- Part of Meta ecosystem — significant overlap with Facebook

#### YouTube
- Profile Photo: 800 x 800
- Banners: 2,048 x 1,152
- Video thumbnail: 1,280 x 720 (16:9 ratio), under 2MB
- Logo on video: white stacked logo bottom right
- Primary channel for being seen outside of in-person performances
- Maintain consistency between video thumbnails within a playlist

#### X (Twitter)
- Profile Photo: 400 x 400
- Header Photos: 1,500 x 500
- Largely text-based — needs to follow Revv52 brand voice
- Watermark photos where possible

#### TikTok
- Profile Photo: 20 x 20
- Video: 1,080 x 1,920
- Video-based — watermark videos where possible

### Website

- **Home Page:** Group image, 2,000 x 900, under 800kb
- **Upcoming Shows:** 2,000 x 480 for banner, square for page
- **Member Photos:** Prefer square, can be rectangular
- Use logo, colour palette, and brand voice consistently

## Logo Usage

- Default logo colour: **solid black**
- Minimum clearance: half the height of the logo's lowercase letters on all sides
- Include the tagline **"Canada's Vocal Energy"** whenever possible
- Omit the tagline if the logo is smaller than 1" wide
- The logo was created using **Trade Gothic Bold** (available through Adobe) — use this font when aligning text with the logo specifically

---

### Quick Reference Sizes
- **Square (1:1):** works for most posts
- **Landscape (16:9):** max 1920×1080
- **Portrait (9:16):** max 1080×1920
- **Facebook Banner:** 1200×628
- **YouTube Banner:** 1280×1152

### Platform-Specific Notes

**Facebook** — Profile photo 170×170, cover photo 1200×628. Use Revv52 logo as watermark on Revv-owned photos. Facebook's branding changes frequently; stay on top of layout updates.

**Instagram** — Profile 320×320. Post images 1080 wide; preferred ratio is square (1:1). Videos typically 20–30 second clips. Part of Meta, so much overlap with Facebook but oriented more toward images and video (including IGTV).

**YouTube** — Profile 800×800, banner 2048×1152, video thumbnail 1280×720 (16:9, under 2 MB). Logo on video: white stacked logo, bottom right. Stay consistent with fonts within a playlist.

**Website** — Home page hero: group cast image, 2000×900, under 800 KB. Show banners: 2000×480. Member photos: square preferred.

### Logo on Social
- Use as a watermark on Revv52-owned photos where possible
- On video: white stacked logo, bottom right corner

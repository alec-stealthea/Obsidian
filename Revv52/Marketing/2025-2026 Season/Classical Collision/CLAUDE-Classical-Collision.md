# Classical Collision

## Key Details

- **Tagline**: Classics Rock — Classic Rock
- **Venue**: Mount Royal University Bella Concert Hall
- **Dates**: May 7 & 8, 2026
- **Guest band**: Jade Elephant (Calgary)

## Show Concept

Two worlds collide in one epic night. In **Classics Rock … Classic Rock**, Revv52 returns to its roots — classical masterpieces reimagined with the full force of a rock band and 52 voices on mics.

**Act I — Classical reimagined**: The majesty of Mozart, the drama of Verdi, the primal power of Carl Orff — all reinterpreted with the drive and edge of modern rock. Soaring harmonies, bold arrangements, and a fusion of styles that makes the old feel thrillingly new.

**Act II — Classic rock**: Calgary's own powerhouse band Jade Elephant joins Revv52 to tear into the legends of classic rock — music that defined generations, performed with all the fire and finesse Revv52 is known for.

## Tone for This Show

Epic, bold, genre-defying. Lean into the collision/contrast angle — centuries of music crashing together. This is the season finale and should feel like an event.

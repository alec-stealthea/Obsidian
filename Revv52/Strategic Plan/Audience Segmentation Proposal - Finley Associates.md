# Audience Segmentation Proposal — Finley & Associates

**Date:** March 21, 2025
**From:** Donna Finley, PhD, MBA, CMC — Finley & Associates Ltd.
**To:** Joe Moreau, Business Manager, Revv52
**Cost:** $14,500 (includes $3,200 non-profit discount)

## Our Understanding

Revv52, established in 1952 as the Calgary Choral Society, is Calgary's oldest continually running vocal ensemble. Rebranded in 2008 to reflect a transition from traditional choral performances to popular music, the group comprises 52 auditioned vocalists who perform alongside a live band, delivering dynamic productions that blend elements of pop and rock. Known for their high-energy shows, Revv52 has performed at prestigious venues such as Carnegie Hall in New York City and the Calgary Stampede Grandstand.

The leadership team is looking to develop a deeper understanding of its core and growth audience segments in order to position itself for the future.

## Objectives

1. **Needs & Expectations** — Determine the existing needs and expectations of the audience with respect to Revv52's programs and services quality.
2. **Needs-based Segmentation** — Validate, size and describe the hypothesized needs-based segments as the foundation for a targeted [[Revv52 Marketing Plan 2025-2026 (Updated)|Marketing Plan]] and the next round of strategic planning.
3. **Value** — Determine the relative value the audience attaches to various program and service offerings, and perceived general value relative to factors such as price.
4. **Satisfaction** — Understand the satisfaction drivers and sub-drivers regarding program and service offerings and communications.
5. **Communication and Engagement** — Facilitate and encourage growth in audience participation and communication.
6. **Trending and Benchmarks** — Establish a baseline for future comparisons.

## Proposed Project Steps

### 1. Planning Meeting (April 2025)

1-hour online planning meeting with a working group to confirm project objectives, steps, timelines, session dates and participants. Discuss pre-work and session materials.

### 2. Review Segmentation Model and Refine Questionnaire

Together with the working group, develop and describe an initial audience needs-based segmentation model based on Finley's arts experience and Revv52 input. The model will be tested through a survey of known supporters (database of ~3,000). Purpose: surface issues for inclusion and develop/refine initial segmentation hypothesis. Finley drafts survey instrument based on input.

### 3. Online Survey

Internet-based survey distributed by email using the Revv52 database of ~3,000 known contacts.

**Process:**
- Email from ED informing respondents of the survey and authorizing Finley
- Email invitation from Finley with survey link, introduction explaining purpose and confidentiality
- Friendly reminder ~1 week before survey closes to non-completers
- Final reminder in last days of the survey

### 4. Data Smoothing & Analysis

Comprehensive analysis including:
- Complete statistical analysis (descriptive statistics and distributions) for each survey item
- Importance/performance domain analysis to identify areas of agitation
- Satisfaction and/or loyalty index development (if applicable)
- Core driver identification using correlation analysis
- Cross-tabulation of performance data against demographic, attitudinal, and behaviouristic variables
- Complete listing of qualitative/open-ended responses
- Implications and recommendations

### 5. Recommendations, Report & Presentation

Final report summarizing major findings with recommendations. 1.5-hour formal presentation to Revv52 key representatives.

## Deliverables

1. A **Segmentation Model** including amalgamated criteria matrix and high-level segment descriptions
2. **4-6 discrete segmentation profiles** with met and unmet needs and demographic characterization
3. **Conclusions** regarding segments for Revv52 to prioritize focus and decision-making
4. A **final report**

## Key Outcomes

1. Increase Board and Management's understanding of its audience for better decision-making
2. Enable ongoing refresh and development of programming, marketing, fundraising, and service support best suited to core and growth audience segments

# Values

Values are fundamental principles and beliefs that serve as implicit criteria guiding all actions and decision-making conducted by Revv52.

•       **Creativity**
•       **Connection**
•       **Excellence**
•       **Collaboration**
•       **Community**

# Vision

The vision articulates the desired future state (“North Star”) of Revv52:

**_Canada’s leader in innovative vocal performance._**

# Mission

The mission is a statement of Revv52’s distinctive identity in the sector:

**_Bring together exceptional performers to create vocal experiences that entertain, engage, and inspire._**

Key Concepts:

- Using innovative technology including individual microphones that let audiences hear every voice and the full power of the choir, we reimagine what choral performance can be.
- Our dynamic repertoire, energy, and sense of fun reflect a member-driven ensemble committed to creating and sustaining Canada’s most innovative vocal arts community.

# Strategic Priorities

The strategic priorities are the long-term focus areas of the group to realize its Mission. All activity undertaken by Revv52 must directly support these priorities: 

- **Organizational sustainability** 
- **Artistic excellence and innovation**
- **Audience development and expansion**

Our goals reflect a focus on the next 12-24 months to materially affect the success of Revv52 in the direction of our Strategic Priorities. These goals are the consensus reflecting the most important efforts of the group, with tangible measures to demonstrate progress. 

## **Goal 1 — By September 2026, Revv52 will finalize and implement a comprehensive organizational sustainability package.** 

### Key Success Factors:

- A capital replacement plan to proactively manage equipment requirements.
- Succession plans for all key roles to ensure sustainable leadership and professional support.
- Improved member engagement reporting to support our growth.
- Success Measures:

	- Capital allocations for each year approved in the annual budget and reconciled through published financial statements.
	- Succession plans completed and approved for all six identified roles.
	- Member satisfaction baseline established using a third-party survey.

### Supports Strategic Priorities: 1A, 1B, 1C

## **Goal 2 — By December 2026, Revv52 will increase member engagement and reduce burnout of more active volunteers.**

### Key Success Factors:

- A third-party survey with >80% response rate to identify any member-wide issues earlier.
- Quarterly skill-building opportunities to provide more meaningful member artistic development.
- A clear volunteer/committee onboarding process to entice new volunteers.
### Success Measures:

- At least 5 new volunteers joining committees or the boards in the 2026-27 season.\
- Reduced regrettable turnover compared with previous season.
- >80% of members participating in announced artistic development 
### Supports Strategic Priorities: 1B, 2A, 2B

## **Goal 3 — By December 2026, Revv52 will implement an enhanced and focused marketing and outreach strategy.**

### Key Success Factors:

- Develop a comprehensive marketing plan based on targeting Revv52’s strategic audience segments that includes actions related to branding, awareness, ticket sales, and audience support.
- Create a job description for professional marketing support to lead and assist Revv52 marketing committee volunteers.

### Success Measures:

- Improve cycle time to 80% capacity to 3 weeks prior to the show.
- Increased overall ticket revenues as a comparative to previous years.
- Improved segment penetration as measured by audience surveys. 
### Supports Strategic Priorities: 3A

## **Goal 4 — By May 2027, Revv52 will develop and deliver even more innovative, highimpact performances.**

Key Success Factors:

•       Finalize show planning at least 6 months earlier than previous years to better inform our members, contractors, musicians and marketing teams.

•       Secure two new arrangers (one non–a cappella focused) to expand creative choices.

•       Compose and perform one original Revv52 signature piece to enhance our originality with audiences.

•       Complete a technology evaluation / capital replacement plan to better understand potential artistic leverage.

•       Implement new creative staging and concert formats.

Success Measures:

•       Production “back-end” workflow completed at least 20% faster.

•       Two new arrangers onboarded and contributing to repertoire.

•       Signature original piece performed publicly at least once.

•       Technology review presented to Board with next-step actions.

•       A compiled list of alternative staging and concert formats/venues 

## Supports Strategic Priorities: 1A, 2A, 2B

**Goal 5 — By May 2027, Revv52 will significantly grow its digital footprint.**

Key Success Factors:

•       Tripling our current email list and quadrupling our social media engagement by July 31, 2027, targeted at our key audience segments to accelerate ticket sales in our current venue.

•       Develop a video/audio content plan to enhance our overall brand image.

Success Measures:

•       Over 10,000 people on our email list, and over 15,000 connections on social media.

•       Minimum of 6 high-quality video/shorts posted by July 31, 2027.

•       At least 4, but preferably 8 (album) of new tracks added to streaming platforms.

_Supports Strategic Priorities: 2B, 3A._

## Core Values and Guiding Principles

As a group we value creativity, diversity, excellence, collaboration, and community.

## Strategic Look Back

Reflections from the 2020 strategic planning process:

- We can be even more ambitious and intentional with our work — not more volume, simply increasing intentionality.
- We need to be more mindful of the capacity and diversity of our members.
- We need clearer communication, increased transparency, and better engagement with our membership.

## The Road to 2025: Three Strategic Goals

### Strategic Goal 1 — Increasing Diversification and Discovery

We will increase the diversity of our membership and who we serve as we move forward with new people, audiences, experiences, and marketing. Diversification will become part of Revv52's DNA and part of our brand.

#### Initiative 1: Internal and External Diversification

Increase the diversity of our membership. Reach out to diverse communities through our membership, alumni, and artists we've worked with before to diversify our brand.

**Key Measures (Internal):**
- In 3 years, membership is more diverse with BIPOC representation at 1:3 (from 1:8)
- 20-30% of applicants are from BIPOC communities
- Diversification includes band, not just singers

**Key Measures (External):**
- Establish one new community relationship per year
- In 3 years, relationships with 3 communities

**Key Actions:**

| Action | Who | When |
|--------|-----|------|
| Activate talent scouts among members and alumni | Board — call to action | Jul 2022 |
| Develop marketing strategies for recruitment (e.g., audition posters in churches) | Marketing with D&I Committee | Jul 2022 |
| Identify other communities to reach out to over next three years | D&I Committee | Oct 2022 |

#### Initiative 2: Increased Collaboration with Different Cultural Communities

**Key Measures:** Conduct two safe-to-fail micro performances per year within new communities or cultural groups.

**Key Actions:**

| Action | Who | When |
|--------|-----|------|
| Reach out to Dr Gupta | Ken Wilson | Sep 2022 |
| Identify 2 more communities to collaborate with | Board and D&I Committee | By 2024 |

#### Initiative 3: Create a Diversity and Inclusion Advisory Committee

**Key Measures:** Committee established and functioning, providing oversight on internal and external diversity.

**Key Actions:**

| Action | Who | When |
|--------|-----|------|
| Clear mandate from Board — roles, objectives, vision. Member present at auditions | Board | Sep 2022 |

### Strategic Goal 2 — Enhancing Collaboration and Relationship

Revv52 will continue to excel by expanding the variety and diversity of its creative elements by collaborating and partnering with both professional artists and organizations and with emerging artists and community events.

#### Initiative 4: Formation of a Relationship Team

Create a Relationship Team at the Board level with capacity to identify and coordinate with individuals and organizations with similar values.

**Key Measures:**
- Collaborate with 3 professional artistic organizations in 3 years
- Collaborate with 3 emerging artists/organizations in 3 years
- Collaborate with an established Artist on a Revv show

**Key Actions:**

| Action | Who | When |
|--------|-----|------|
| Form Relationship Team at Board level including full-time staff person | Board / Artistic Director | Sep 2022 |
| Identify 1 potential opportunity for collaboration | Relationship Team | Nov 2022 |
| Develop and nurture relationship leading to collaborative performance | Relationship Team / AD | Summer 2023 |

#### Initiative 5: Actively Seek Out Relationships

With potential entities including artists, music-based organizations, events (not just N. America), venues, corporations, sponsors.

**Key Measures:**
- Collaborate with 1-3 professional organizations outside Calgary in 5 years
- Collaborate with a cultural entity in 2 years
- Engage in social causes aligned with values

#### Initiative 6: Inter-group Collaboration

Bring voices and skill sets to an inter-group.

### Strategic Goal 3 — Member Development

Revv52 will increase the development and engagement level of its membership through coaching, workshops, and masterclasses while increasing the level of communication and connection.

#### Initiative 7: Enhance Vocal Coaching

Expand the role of vocal coaches to choose selections for singers, combine vocalists for projects, and curate material for performances.

**Key Measures:** Member confidence increases; coaches play a more significant role in shaping vocal performances.

**Key Actions:**

| Action | Who | When |
|--------|-----|------|
| Change the coaching process | Artistic Director | End of Q4 2022 |
| Micro-shows are coach curated | Artistic Director | End of Q4 2022 |

#### Initiative 8: Workshops and Master Classes

Workshops: staging, movement, "selling the song", acting, wardrobe, vocal range/technique. Masterclasses: theory, harmonizing, pitch, sight reading.

**Key Measures:** Solid attendance/engagement; performance skills improved.

**Key Actions:**

| Action | Who | When |
|--------|-----|------|
| Contact coaches to determine Masterclass preferences | Artistic Director | End of Q2 2022 |
| Poll membership for key interests | Artistic Director | End of Q3 2022 |
| Deliver 2 workshops per season | Artistic Director | End of Q2 2023 |
| Deliver 2 Masterclasses per season | Artistic Director | End of Q2 2023 |

**Delivery Options:**
1. Host in-house, led by AD, vocal coaches, and members
2. Send all singers to established external professional session
3. Send representative to external session who brings learning back

#### Initiative 9: Communication and Connection

Create a more inclusive and transparent culture by enhancing transparency in decision making and quality of communication.

**Key Measures:** More buy-in to decisions; fewer questions/confusion; eliminate cliques and perceived favouritism.

# CLAUDE-Revv52.md — Revv52 Context

> **Purpose**: Context for all Revv52 work — a non-profit choral performance group in Calgary that Alec serves as a board member and marketing committee chair.

---

## About Revv52

Revv52 is a high-energy vocal performance group rooted in popular music — not a stuffy classical choir. 52 singers on mics performing rock, pop, and genre-crossing shows with live bands. Based in Calgary, Alberta.

## Strategic Framework

The [[Revv52 2026-2030 Strategic Framework]] defines the current strategic direction. Core values: Creativity, Connection, Excellence, Collaboration, Community. Vision: "Canada's leader in innovative vocal performance."

## Alec's Roles

Board member and marketing committee chair (meets 4th Tuesday monthly). See root [[CLAUDE]] for Alec's full profile.

The practice of managing an organisation's collection of applications as a portfolio of investments, balancing costs, risks, and business value. Application portfolio management involves cataloguing applications, assessing their health and strategic fit, and making decisions about investment, consolidation, or retirement.

Within Stealth EA, applications are TIERed as Top, Innovation, Enabling, or Retire - providing a simple framework for portfolio decisions.

In dimensional modelling, facts are the numeric measurements of business processes - the things you count, sum, or average. Revenue, quantity sold, hours worked, patients treated. Facts live in fact tables and are typically additive (you can sum them up meaningfully).

Facts answer the "how much" or "how many" questions. They're the numbers that executives want on their dashboards, which is precisely why getting them right matters so much.

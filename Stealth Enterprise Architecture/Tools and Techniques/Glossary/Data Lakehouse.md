A data lakehouse is an architecture that combines the flexibility of a data lake (storing raw, unstructured data cheaply) with the structure and query performance of a data warehouse (optimized for analytics). Think of it as having your cake and eating it too - you get the "dump everything here" convenience of a lake with the "actually find what you need" usability of a warehouse.

Unlike traditional approaches where you had to choose between a data lake or a data warehouse, the lakehouse lets you store raw data and progressively refine it into analytics-ready datasets - all in one place.

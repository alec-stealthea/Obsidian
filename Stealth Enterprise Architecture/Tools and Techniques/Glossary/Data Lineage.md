The documented trail of how data has been transformed as it moves through systems and processes. While [[Data Provenance]] tells you where data was born, data lineage tells you everywhere it's been and what happened to it along the way.

Think of it as the travel history for your data. When a calculation seems wrong, lineage helps you understand which transformations, joins, or aggregations might have introduced the issue.

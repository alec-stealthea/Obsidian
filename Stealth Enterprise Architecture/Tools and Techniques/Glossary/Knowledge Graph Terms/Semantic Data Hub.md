This may be a semantic graph or property graph, and serves to provide a way to transform data information from Smartone ontology (or data schema) to another. Most such data hubs are not as focused on human use as they are on providing a bridge between various system representations. They usually work in conjunction with a semantic data catalog, with the catalog identifying the resources to work with and the data hub then taking the appropriate transformations from the catalog to convert to a common canonical model and out again, in order to minimize combinatoric explosion. It should be pointed out that any transformation is likely to be lossy. This is an area where machine language and even more sophisticated AI will likely play a bigger part in the future.

ArchiMate definition, "The **Flow Relationship** represents transfer (e.g. of information, goods or money) from one element to another."

From a Stealth Enterprise Architecture perspective, this relationship becomes very important for understanding the information flow at the Business Application level which is usually represented as an Application Component in ArchiMate.

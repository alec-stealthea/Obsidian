Within ArchiMate a Business Role, "represents the responsibility for performing specific behaviour, to which an actor can be assigned, or the part an actor plays in a particular action or event."

From the [[Stealth EA Metamodel]], we maintain the relationship linking a Business Actor to a role. A Business Role also is usually linked to both a Business Domain as well as Stakeholder, usually associated with an Organization.

Within ArchiMate a Business Function, "represents a collection of business behaviour based on a chosen set of criteria such as required business resources and/or competencies, and is managed or performed as a whole."

For the Stealth EA, we have selected Business Function to represent the concept of a Business Domain.  This may be a contentious choice within the Enterprise Architecture community. There is a long-standing argument on what the difference between a business capability and a business function and if they really are the same thing.  

While even Stealth EA's can appreciate a great semantic and methodological debate, for Stealth EA an ArchiMate Business Function = Business Domain which is not a Business Capability!

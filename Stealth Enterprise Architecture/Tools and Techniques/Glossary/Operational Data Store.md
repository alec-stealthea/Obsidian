A database designed to integrate data from multiple sources for operational reporting and light analytics. Unlike a data warehouse (optimised for historical analysis), an operational data store focuses on current or near-current data for day-to-day decision making.

Many applications maintain their own operational data stores separate from their production databases - nobody wants a rogue report query bringing down the order entry system at 2 PM on a Friday.

OKR stands for “Objectives and Key Results.” It is a goal-setting framework used by individuals, teams, and organizations to define and track measurable goals. An OKR is made up of:

• **Objective:** A clear, inspiring, and often qualitative goal that sets the direction (what you want to achieve).
• **Key Results:** Specific, measurable markers of progress that indicate whether you have successfully met the objective (how you know you’ve achieved it).

OKRs help create focus, alignment, and accountability by ensuring everyone pursues shared objectives and measures success using concrete results. Within ArchiMate an OCR would usually be represented an ArchiMate Goal with related ArchiMate Outcomes.

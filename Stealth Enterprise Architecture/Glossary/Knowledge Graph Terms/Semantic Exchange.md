The use of knowledge graphs to facilitate the exchange of information, products, or services, either freely given or for a price. Exchanges are only lately becoming feasible, given performance and design considerations of tuple stores, but generally rely upon the rich interplay of metadata surrounding buyers, sellers, products, and jurisdictions.

An Entity Relationship Diagram (ERD) is a visual representation of the relationships between business or information objects.  At the Business Object level this would be a conceptual models that defines the relationships between entities. At the Information Object level a logical ERD adds the attributes of the Information Object and more specific primary and foreign keys that relate the information objects together.

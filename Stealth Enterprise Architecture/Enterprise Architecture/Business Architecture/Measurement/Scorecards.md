Most businesses love thier scorecards.  The question is - are they really useful?  Annual reports are a great example.  Lots of numbers that show how a business is doing against past performance, or possibly against competitors or general industry averages. 

For the Stealth EA, to be effective, measures should be grounded in value streams and then expanded to value stream networks.  Naturally, most organizations organize around value streams.  This helps and hurts in that we end up with high level measures. Let's take our Pizzaria example.  Customers would like to know when their pizza will be ready.  Some may set an arbitrary measure like 30 minutes.  Others may look at current order volume and the throughput formula they have created.

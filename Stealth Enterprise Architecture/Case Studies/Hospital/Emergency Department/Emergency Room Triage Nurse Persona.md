### **Persona: ER Triage Nurse**

**Name**: Clara Hughes (example persona name)
**Role**: Emergency Room Triage Nurse
**Domain**: Healthcare - Emergency Services

#### **Characteristics:**

• **Skills and Certifications**: Clara is a registered nurse (RN) with certifications in Advanced Cardiac Life Support (ACLS), Pediatric Advanced Life Support (PALS), and Trauma Nursing Core Course (TNCC). She is proficient in handling critical care and managing high-stress environments.
• **Work Environment**: Works in a fast-paced, high-stress hospital emergency room. She is required to make quick decisions regarding patient conditions and prioritize them based on severity.
• **Proficiency in Tools**: Clara uses electronic health records (EHR) systems to document patient information and is trained in the use of various medical equipment (e.g., defibrillators, oxygen tanks, and monitoring devices). She also uses triage algorithms to guide decision-making.
• **Shifts**: Works rotating 12-hour shifts, often including nights and weekends.  
#### **Day in the Life:**

  • **Morning**: Clara starts her shift by reviewing patient logs from the previous shift and preparing herself mentally for the unpredictable nature of the ER. She gets a handover from the previous triage nurse and prepares to receive incoming patients.
• **Mid-day**: The ER is typically at its busiest. Clara assesses patients, assigns triage categories (e.g., urgent, semi-urgent), and communicates with other healthcare professionals. She documents each patient’s vitals and symptoms in the EHR and manages patient flow efficiently.
• **Afternoon/Evening**: By the end of her shift, Clara updates patient statuses, ensuring those still in the ER are receiving the appropriate care. She completes documentation and provides a handover to the next nurse on duty.
#### **Persona Goals and Objectives:**

##### **Experience Goals**:
• Clara wants to ensure patients are treated fairly and quickly, while minimizing wait times.
• She prefers clear communication tools and an efficient workflow that reduces stress and bottlenecks in the triage process.
#####  **Organizational Goals**:
• Clara’s role supports the hospital’s goal of reducing patient wait times and improving overall patient satisfaction.
• She contributes to meeting quality metrics like timely patient assessment and adherence to emergency care protocols.
##### **Life Goals**:

• As a mid-career professional, Clara aims to further her education, potentially moving into nurse management or specialized emergency care.
• Work-life balance is important to her, and she hopes to eventually move to a day-shift-only role to accommodate family time.
